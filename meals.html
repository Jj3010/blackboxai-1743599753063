Meal Prep Tips

Take time each week to plan your meals. This helps with grocery shopping and ensures you have all ingredients ready. Consider preparing meals that use similar ingredients to minimize waste.

Cook large portions of staple foods like grains, proteins, and roasted vegetables that can be mixed and matched throughout the week. Store them in separate containers to maintain freshness.

Use airtight containers to keep food fresh longer. Store dressings and sauces separately to prevent sogginess. Label containers with dates to track freshness.
